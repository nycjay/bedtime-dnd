📖 Dungeon Master's Guide

Getting Started

{{ app_name }} is a bedtime D&D app for families. One person runs the game from their phone — the AI is your Dungeon Master, telling the story and reacting to what the players do.

  1. Create an account — tap "Sign up" on the login screen
  2. Create characters — go to the Characters tab and make one for each kid
  3. Create a campaign — give it a name, pick a theme and difficulty
  4. Add characters to the party — on the campaign page, select who's playing
  5. Hit Play! — the adventure begins

Characters

Each kid gets their own character with a name, class, stats, and optional avatar.

  • 🧙 Classes — Warrior, Wizard, Rogue, Healer, Ranger, Elf Knight, Dwarf Smith, Druid, Bard, Paladin, Dragon, Dragonborn, Fairy, Beastmaster, Pirate, Clockwork Knight, or make your own
  • 📊 Stats — Might, Agility, Wits (9 points total). These affect the story.
  • ❤️ Max HP — calculated automatically (8 + Might)
  • 📸 Avatar — upload a photo and AI generates an 80s fantasy portrait
  • 📝 Description — backstory and traits that the DM weaves into the narrative
  • ⬆️ Leveling Up — the DM awards XP for heroic moments, clever ideas, and great rolls. At 3 XP, characters level up automatically and earn +1 stat point to allocate on the character page.

Playing the Game

  • 🎲 Dice — when the DM asks for a roll, the dice button glows. Tap it and the roll is sent automatically.
  • 💬 Actions — type what the heroes do ("We sneak past the guard" or "I open the chest")
  • 🎒 Inventory — tap a character's avatar in the HP bar to see their items
  • 🖼️ Scene Art — dramatic moments may generate an illustration inline
  • 🌙 Bedtime — tap the moon button to wrap up for the night
  • 📖 Quest Summary — forgot what happened? Tap for a full recap.
  • ⏮️ Previously On — quick recap of the last session.
  • 🔊 Read Aloud — tap the speaker icon to hear the DM narrate. Tap again to stop.
  • 📱 Follow Along — open the same quest on another device to watch live
  • 🔀 Party Order — on the campaign page, use ▲/▼ arrows to set the order. The DM addresses characters in this order.

Health Points (HP)

  • ❤️ Max HP — each character starts with 8 + Might. Visible in the top bar during play.
  • ⚔️ Taking damage — the DM deals damage during combat or dangerous situations based on dice rolls.
  • 💚 Healing — potions, spells, or rest can restore HP during the adventure.
  • 💤 Long Rest — when you come back for the next session (4+ hours later), everyone's HP is fully restored automatically. No need to worry about ending on low health!
  • 💀 At 0 HP — depends on your content rating. At Campfire, it won't happen. At Quest, heroes get knocked out and can be rescued. At Dragon's Lair, it's serious.
  • 🎭 Adventure Theme — pick a preset (Dungeon Crawl, Rescue Mission, etc.) or write your own
  • ⚙️ Difficulty — Easy, Normal, Hard (affects how tough challenges are)
  • 🏕️ Content Rating:
    • Campfire — cozy, no danger, silly fun
    • Quest — danger exists, heroes get knocked out but not killed
    • Dragon's Lair — high stakes, real consequences

Playing with Another Family

You can share a campaign with another household and take turns!

  1. The other family signs up at the app
  2. Set your display name in Settings (so they recognize you)
  3. Add them as a friend in Settings (enter their email)
  4. On your campaign page, tap their name to share — one tap!
  5. Both families add their characters to the party
  6. Take turns — after you play, it's their turn next

Tips for Bedtime

  • ⏱️ Sessions work best at 15-20 minutes
  • 🌙 Use the bedtime button when it's time to stop
  • 📱 Open the app a minute early so it's ready
  • 🎭 Let the kids make choices — the DM adapts to anything
  • 🎲 Young kids love rolling dice — let them tap the button even for small things
  • 🔊 Pick a narrator voice in Settings — great for kids still learning to read
